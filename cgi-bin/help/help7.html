Layout.
One of the easiest to understand but hard to learn, mmm, doesn't mean you shouldn't. So... Here is a quick info page about our layout.

1. My amount of bullets, it is green because I have some bullets.
2. The Boss's (my opponent) amount of bullets, it is red because he doesn't have any bullets.
3. What move The Boss performed in this round.
4. The round message, it has a brief commentry on this round.
5. Row of buttons. (sometimes 'Shoot' is greyed out because you don't have any bullets.
6. What move I performed in this round.
